# Task C — Revised Requirements

This section contains corrected and improved requirements to resolve the defects identified in Task A.

---

## 1. Registration Deadline Rule (Revised R2 & R3)

The system shall allow course registration until the official registration deadline.
Late registration shall be permitted for up to 3 calendar days after the deadline only with administrative approval.

---

## 2. Seat Availability Rule (Revised R4 & R5)

The system shall display the total and available seats for each course.
If seats are full, the system shall prevent direct registration and provide a waitlist option.

---

## 3. Schedule Conflict Rule (Revised R9 & R10)

The system shall prevent students from registering for courses with overlapping schedules.
An exception shall apply only if one of the courses is fully online and does not require live attendance.

---

## 4. Credit Hour Limit Rule (Revised R11 & R12)

The system shall limit students to a maximum of 18 credit hours per semester.
Students may register for up to 24 credit hours only after receiving digital approval from their academic advisor before final submission.

---

## 5. Course Drop Policy (Revised R13 & R15)

The system shall allow students to drop courses until the end of Week 6 of the semester.
Course drops shall not be permitted after Week 6.

---

## 6. Drop Fee Rule (Revised R14)

The system shall charge a fixed drop fee of $50 for any course dropped after Week 2 and before the end of Week 6.

---

## 7. Confirmation Requirement (Revised R6)

The system shall send an email confirmation to the student within 1 minute after successful registration, waitlisting, or course drop.

---

## 8. Instructor Approval Rule (Revised R8)

The system shall allow prerequisite override only after the instructor submits digital approval through the system portal.

---

## 9. Waitlist Rule (Revised R16–R19)

The system shall maintain a waitlist when course seats are full.
Waitlisted students shall be automatically enrolled when a seat becomes available.
Enrollment priority shall follow this order:
1. Final-year students
2. Other students based on waitlist joining time (first-come, first-served).

---

## 10. Performance Requirement (Revised R20)

The system shall process course registration requests within 3 seconds under normal operating conditions.

---

# Summary

All revised requirements are:
- Clear and specific
- Free from conflicts
- Measurable and testable
- Complete with defined rules and processes
